# MPP-100
## Constitution of Meridian Press

**Publication ID:** MPP-100

**Publication Family:** Meridian Press Publications

**Status:** Canon Approved

**Authority:** Meridian Constitution

## Purpose
Meridian Press is established as the official publishing and editorial institution of the Meridian Project. It preserves, communicates, organises and publishes the architectural knowledge of Meridian while remaining subordinate to the Meridian Constitution and Canon.

## Constitutional Authority
Meridian Press derives all authority from the Meridian Constitution and possesses no independent constitutional authority.

## Editorial Principles
- Understanding before implementation.
- Accuracy before speed.
- Clarity before complexity.
- Constitutional consistency.
- Traceability to the Canon.
- Historical continuity.

## Constitutional Declaration
Meridian Press exists to illuminate the Canon, preserve architectural understanding, and ensure every publication strengthens the continuity and integrity of the Meridian Project.
